ZX spectrum games

To play the games you must download some zx spectrum emulator.

KULILI 


Simple ala pacman game with just one predictable enemy.
100 levels.
Passwords are 00001,00006,00011,...,00096

keys: Q,A,O,P,M

download:kulili.sna

QIKULI 2 


Logic game, move over white boxes to remove them all.
Go through them up.
50 levels.
Shift+jump to abort level.
Passwords are 000000,050000,100000,...,450000

download:qikuli_2.sna

BYKULI 


Shooting game with blocking enemies.
38 levels.

download:bykuli.sna
